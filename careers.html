Why Sea Eagles

The team behind 1,200+ projects

Working at SES means working on owned vessels with stable rotations, structured development and the kind of safety culture that shows up in the small decisions, not just the big ones.

Competitive Pay

Sea-time and shore-side compensation benchmarked to the regional market, with transparent contracts and on-time payment.

Global Operations

Active assignments across the Middle East, Africa, Asia and Europe — work the world without leaving the family of one operator.
