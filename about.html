Our identity

An integrated marine operator, not a broker

Sea Eagles Group of Companies (SES) was founded in the UAE in 1984. Our Saudi sister company, Zaki Alzayer Marine Services, was established in 2011 in Dammam. Together, the two legacies operate as one group — owner of 30+ vessels, employer of 5,000+ team members, and a trusted partner on more than 1,200 marine projects across four continents.

Headquartered at Plot HD 09 A in Hamriyah Free Zone, Sharjah, with regional hubs in Saudi Arabia, Bahrain and Singapore, we serve clients across the Middle East, Africa, Asia and Europe.

Our journey

Four decades on the water

Three milestones that built the group as it stands today — and the operating discipline that runs through every contract we sign.

1984

Sea Eagles Group founded in the UAE

Established as a maritime services operator in Sharjah, focused initially on tug and supply work for the rapidly growing UAE oil & gas and construction sectors.

2011

Zaki Alzayer Marine Services launched in Saudi Arabia

The Saudi sister company opened in Dammam, extending the SES platform into the Eastern Province and deepening regional capability across the Arabian Gulf.

Today

30+ vessels, 5,000+ team, four continents

SES now operates an owner-managed fleet across six integrated service divisions, with offices in UAE, Saudi Arabia, Bahrain and Singapore — and active projects spanning the Middle East, Africa, Asia and Europe.
