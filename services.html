What we do · Six divisions

Marine services built on trust

From chartering a single crew boat to managing a full offshore campaign, six integrated divisions operate under one banner — and one point of accountability.

01 · Vessel Chartering

Crew boats, tugs & supply vessels on demand

Charter solutions for crew boats, towing tugs, ASD tugs and supply vessels tailored to project requirements across the Arabian Gulf and beyond. Short-term spot charter, term charter, or bareboat — we structure agreements that match your operational and commercial needs.

  • Spot & term charter for crew boats up to 42 m
  • ASD harbour tug deployment for terminal & berth assistance
  • Supply vessel operations for offshore platforms
  • Pilot boat services for port authorities
Browse Fleet →
02 · Marine Management & Consultancy

Full vessel management, end-to-end

Crew coordination, maintenance planning, operational oversight, classification and flag-state compliance — we manage vessels with the same standards we apply to our own fleet.

  • Crew sourcing, training and rotation management
  • Technical & planned-maintenance programmes
  • Bureau Veritas / class society liaison
  • Flag state compliance & certification
  • Budget control and operational reporting
03 · Marine Agency Services

Port agency across UAE, Saudi & Bahrain

Port clearance, crew changes, ship chandlery, customs documentation and port agent representation — our offices across the Gulf give you ground presence wherever your hull is calling.

  • Port call & clearance management
  • Crew change logistics and visa support
  • Ship chandlery, bunkering & provisioning
  • Customs documentation & cargo clearance
  • Husbandry services & vessel inspections
04 · Engineering & Maintenance

In-house workshop, Hamriyah Free Zone

Ship repair, steel fabrication, mechanical and electrical maintenance, and drydocking coordination — delivered from our own facility, on our own timeline.

  • Steel fabrication and structural repair
  • Mechanical, electrical and hydraulic maintenance
  • Drydock planning & project coordination
  • Class survey preparation
  • Conversion & refit projects
05 · Marine Logistics & Supply

Sea, land and warehousing in one flow

Sea and land logistics for offshore operations, equipment transportation, warehouse rental and integrated supply chain management — keeping operations moving across the region.

  • Offshore logistics & equipment transport
  • Free zone warehousing & staging
  • Supply chain consolidation
  • Project cargo handling & heavy-lift coordination
06 · Offshore Construction Support

From platform supply to pilotage

Platform supply, mooring and docking operations, pilotage services and offshore construction consultancy — the offshore support layer for complex marine projects.

  • Offshore platform & rig supply
  • Mooring & berthing assistance
  • Pilotage services for harbours & terminals
  • Subsea installation support consultancy
How we work

A simple three-stage process

01

Request a vessel

Tell us the operational envelope — vessel type, duration, location and crew needs. We respond within one business day with availability and indicative pricing.

02

Fleet deployment

Once terms are agreed, we mobilise crew, complete class and flag formalities and deliver the vessel to your nominated port — fully manned and provisioned.

03

Service completion

From day-to-day operations to demobilisation, the SES operations desk stays close to the project until handover is complete and final reporting is delivered.
